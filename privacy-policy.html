Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Live Scale Detector (“the App”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) handles information when you use our mobile application published on the Google Play Store.

Information We Collect

Live Scale Detector does not collect, store, or require any personally identifiable information.

The app requires access to the following device feature:

  • Microphone – Used only for real-time audio input to detect musical notes and scales.

All audio processing happens locally on your device. Audio is not recorded, stored, or transmitted.

Permissions

  • Microphone permission is required strictly for the core functionality of live scale detection.

The app does not use the camera or access any visual data.

Advertising

Live Scale Detector uses Google AdMob to display ads.

AdMob may collect certain information automatically, including but not limited to:

  • Device information (such as device type, OS version)
  • Advertising ID
  • Approximate location (non-precise)
  • Ad interaction data

This information is used by Google to provide and improve advertising services. We do not have access to, control over, or store this data.

You can learn more about how Google handles data here:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data Sharing

We do not share any user data ourselves. Any data collected is handled directly by Google AdMob under Google’s privacy policies.

Data Storage

The app itself does not store personal or sensitive user data. All audio analysis is transient and discarded immediately after processing.

Children’s Privacy

Live Scale Detector does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Ads shown by Google AdMob comply with Google’s policies for child protection where applicable.

User Choices

Users may opt out of personalized advertising through their device’s Google Ads settings.

Security

We follow a privacy-by-design approach. All core functionality works offline and on-device, minimizing privacy risks.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a revised date.
